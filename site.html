Services

Reliable help for hockey families.

Flying V provides sharpening, profiling, repairs and common hockey supplies, with service options designed to be convenient for busy families.

⛸️

Skate sharpening

Regular hockey sharpening, figure skate sharpening and sharpening tailored to the skater's preferred hollow or FBV.

📐

Custom profiling

Custom skate profiling is listed using Prosharp profile templates, with consultation to match skating style and needs.

🧰

Repairs

Repairs include rivets, eyelets, hooks, holders, patching, Velcro, buckles, goalie gear work and re-palming.

🧼

Gear wash

Equipment cleaning is offered to help keep gear fresher between busy hockey weeks.

🏒

Hockey supplies

Tape, laces, wax, bottles, mouthguards, soft guards, repair kits and other useful rink essentials.

Family-friendly and convenient: The goal is to make sharpening and skate care easy for parents, players and figure skaters without adding extra stress to the week.

Meet the family behind Flying V

Local experience and people who care about the details.

Kevin Bernard was born and raised in Northumberland and Peterborough County. He has played locally as both a forward and goalie, including Junior C with the Colborne Black Hawks, and understands how much trust skaters place in their edges.

When Kevin cannot be at the rink, his dad Eric helps cover the shop. Kevin's daughter Ella brings the perspective of a U18 AA hockey player, and his youngest daughter's competitive figure skating experience helps inform the care given to figure skates.

The goal is straightforward: consistent sharpening tailored to each skater, without needing to “work in” a fresh sharpening on the boards.

Common questions

Simple answers before you go.

Do I need an appointment?
The Cramahe location is open year-round by appointment and offers drop-off service. Message Kevin to confirm availability before heading over.
Does Flying V sharpen figure skates?
Yes. Competitive figure skate sharpening is listed as available by appointment with Kevin.
Can Flying V use my preferred hollow?
Yes. The current site states that skates are sharpened to the customer's preferred radius of hollow or Flat Bottom V, then levelled and de-burred.
Can I book Flying V for a tournament?
Yes. The mobile trailer is listed for tournament bookings across Northumberland County and several surrounding counties.
Is there a loyalty program?
The current site lists loyalty cards with the fifth pair free. Ask Kevin for the current terms when booking.
